FinResilience Pro Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

At FinResilience Pro, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal and financial data you share with us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our automated wealth and debt optimization platform.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our optimization services, we collect several types of information from and about our users:

  1. Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, password, and preferences.
  2. Financial Information: We collect information regarding your debts, assets, income, expenses, interest rates, and financial goals. This information is required for our algorithm to generate optimization strategies.
  3. Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with our application, such as access times, pages viewed, and device type.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your collected information strictly for the following purposes:

  1. To operate, maintain, and provide the features and functionality of the FinResilience Pro platform.
  2. To perform calculations, run debt payoff simulations, and customize asset allocation recommendations.
  3. To send you important notifications regarding account updates, security alerts, and system notices.
  4. To monitor usage statistics and improve platform performance and usability.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

FinResilience Pro does not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information with third parties. We only disclose information under the following limited circumstances:

  1. With your explicit consent or direction to share information with external services.
  2. To comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  3. To protect the rights, property, or safety of FinResilience Pro, our users, or the general public.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal and financial information for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide you with services. You may request the deletion of your account and associated financial data at any time via your account settings page.

5. Security of Your Data

We implement industry-standard administrative, physical, and electronic security measures to safeguard your sensitive information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
